ABOUT

We are a family friendly Karate club based in Royston , Hertfordshire in the UK. Our aim is to teach Shotokan style of karate with emphasis to self-defense and fitness. The classes run in a safe and friendly environment, with paramount importance to safety, respect and discipline; making it comfortable for everyone to learn and enjoy the training.

We have students of all ages, including adult beginners. The club accepts the students from the age of six onwards.
